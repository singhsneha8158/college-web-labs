Learn • Lead • Innovate

Empowering students with skills, research, and values for a better future.

Vision

To be a globally admired center of innovation and excellence in higher education, research, and industry collaboration that fosters holistic development.

Mission

  • Deliver quality education that combines knowledge with practical application.
  • Promote interdisciplinary research and entrepreneurship.
  • Strengthen industry-academic partnerships for global exposure.
  • Inculcate values, ethics, and leadership qualities.

Program Specific Outcomes

PSO 1: Core Competence

Apply computing fundamentals to design, develop, and maintain software solutions.

PSO 2: Emerging Tech

Use AI, ML, IoT, and Cloud technologies to solve industry and societal challenges.

PSO 3: Professional Skills

Demonstrate teamwork, communication, and ethical practices in professional settings.
